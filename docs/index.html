AccessibilityPlus

Making the world an equal playing field.

AccessibilityPlus is a web extension designed to make reading text on websites easier for dyslexic people and elders.
The extension allows you to change font color, font size, link color, and background color.
You could also pick from a few presets that are designed to make your life much easier.
